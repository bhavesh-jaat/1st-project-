Tournament Rules

Please read and follow the tournament rules to ensure fair play and a smooth experience.

General Rules

  • All participants must register using accurate details.
  • Each team must consist of 3-5 players, depending on the tournament format.
  • Players are not allowed to use cheats or third-party software that gives an unfair advantage.
  • Teams are required to have a designated captain who will be responsible for the team's actions.
  • Only players who have signed up and have been approved will be allowed to participate in the tournament.

Gameplay Rules

  • All matches will be played under the official game settings unless stated otherwise.
  • Players must be on time for their scheduled matches. Late teams may be disqualified.
  • If a player disconnects during a match, they have a limited time to reconnect. The match may be paused if agreed by both teams.
  • Each team is required to communicate with the organizers if there are any issues or disputes during the match.
  • The tournament will be conducted in a single-elimination format (unless stated otherwise). Losing teams will be eliminated.

Code of Conduct

  • Respect other players, organizers, and spectators at all times.
  • Any form of harassment, abuse, or toxic behavior will result in disqualification.
  • Players must conduct themselves professionally and represent their team positively.
  • Teams caught violating the code of conduct may face penalties or be disqualified from the event.

Prizes and Rewards

  • The prize pool will be distributed according to the official prize distribution rules.
  • All winners will be notified via email with further instructions on how to claim their prizes.
  • Prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash or other rewards.
  • GameArena reserves the right to modify prize details or cancel the prize offering if necessary.

Disqualifications

  • Teams found violating tournament rules, cheating, or engaging in inappropriate conduct will be disqualified.
  • Failure to show up on time for matches without valid reason will result in disqualification.
  • Any team caught submitting false information during registration may face immediate disqualification.
